WHY?

Quantity of blogs, websites and YouTube clips about hula hooping can be overwhelming for hula hoop beginners. Having our own experiences with getting lost in the Internet when trying to look for one trick only, we decided to put all our knowledge about hula hooping in one place. We hope you find it useful :)
